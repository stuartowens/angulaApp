Lodging Description:

Camp Harrison is a new state of the art YMCA camp, with a wonderful dining hall, great food from their commercially designed kitchen and other great outbuildings we will use to have various activities related to BandCamp.

Options:

There are multiple lodging options available. There are sixteen nice cabins with eight bunks each and each cabin has a private bath area with multiple showers and toilet stalls. Cabins will be divided into "boys and girls" for optimal privacy. There is space to park RV's, no hookups as far as water or sewage, but there are places to plug in for basic power, and there is a tent option also. Two cabins, boys and girls, will be used for the RV and Tent people to use for showers and bathrooms. Since breakfast, dinner and shower access are provided for RV and Tent campers, there is a charge per person for that.

Camp Harrison is about 20 minutes south of Wilkesboro and there is also the option of staying in hotels in Wilkesboro, although we don't recommend it as there will be nighttime activities, homework you might say!
